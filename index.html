Project Description

This website showcases how South African communities use gardening to fight food insecurity. We highlight real examples, offer charts, and provide calculators to help plan and manage small to medium food gardens.

Real Case Studies (South Africa)

  • Emfuleni, Gauteng: 86% of participants could supplement their diets through gardening.
  • Umdoni, KZN: Only 15.8% reached full food security through gardens. Issues included water and training.
  • EduPlant: Schools turned into food production hubs. 450+ schools in 2024.
  • Siyakhana Garden, Johannesburg: Trains communities and promotes sustainability.
